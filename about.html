About Us

Smart Stubble Management

Our Smart Stubble Management System is designed to tackle the environmental issues arising from stubble burning. This common agricultural practice significantly contributes to air pollution, greenhouse gas emissions, and soil degradation. EcoParali offers an innovative solution by transforming stubble waste into valuable resources such as biofuels, organic fertilizers, and animal bedding. By adopting our system, farmers can manage stubble sustainably, reduce their environmental footprint, and improve soil health. Our approach not only addresses immediate ecological concerns but also fosters long-term sustainability and resilience in agricultural practices.

What is ECOPARALI?

Your New Green Solution

EcoParali is a Smart Stubble Management System that provides an innovative and sustainable solution to the environmental challenges posed by stubble burning. Traditionally, farmers burn stubble to clear fields for the next planting season, which results in significant air pollution, greenhouse gas emissions, and soil degradation. EcoParali addresses these issues by converting stubble waste into valuable raw materials through advanced technologies and eco-friendly processes. Our system helps mitigate the harmful effects of stubble burning, promote sustainable agricultural practices, and enhance the overall health of the ecosystem.

Our Team

Meet The Pioneers

The Paryavaran Pioneers are a dynamic team of four engineering students who transformed a real-life problem into actionable solutions. Driven by a passion for environmental sustainability, they conducted extensive ground-level surveys and engaged directly with farmers and gram panchayats to understand the challenges of stubble management. Their hands-on approach and innovative thinking led to the development of EcoParali, a platform designed to provide sustainable alternatives to stubble burning, improve farmers' livelihoods, and promote a greener rural India. Their dedication and ingenuity exemplify how young minds can drive meaningful change and make a lasting impact on both the environment and the community.
